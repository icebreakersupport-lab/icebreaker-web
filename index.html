How it works

Three steps. Zero awkwardness.

Icebreaker is built around one idea: you should only approach someone when you already know the feeling is mutual.

Step 01
🔴

Go Live

Tap Go Live when you're out and want to connect. Your verified profile becomes visible to compatible people nearby. You control when you're discoverable.

Step 02
👥

See who's nearby

Browse real people within metres of you, right now. No endless profile scrolling — just who's actually around you in this moment.

Step 03
⚡

Send an Icebreaker

When interest is mutual, an Icebreaker unlocks your connection. Both people know. No rejection. No guessing. Just a real opening to say hello.

Safety first

Designed to feel
safe from day one.

Real-time dating apps require real-world trust. Every feature in Icebreaker is built with your safety in mind — not as an afterthought.

🛡️ Safety-first by design
📧

Email verification

Every account requires a verified email address before you can go live. Anonymous accounts aren't allowed.

🎥

Live selfie check

Before going live, users complete a real-time selfie verification to confirm they match their profile. No catfishing.

🚫

Block & report

Block or report anyone instantly. Blocked users are removed from your discovery and yours from theirs — immediately.

🔐

You control visibility

You only appear when you choose to go live. The moment you end your session, you disappear from discovery entirely.
