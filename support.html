Common questions

Icebreaker is a real-world dating app for people who are physically in the same space. When you tap "Go Live", your verified profile becomes discoverable to other live users within your chosen radius (30–60 metres). If two people both show interest in each other, an Icebreaker unlocks — giving you both the signal to approach without guessing.
To go live you need to: (1) verify your email address — check your inbox for a verification link, (2) complete the live selfie verification, and (3) have your account fully set up and location access enabled. If your email is verified but you still can't go live, check that location permission is enabled for Icebreaker in your device settings.
After clicking the verification link in your inbox, return to the Icebreaker app. The app should refresh your status automatically within a few seconds. If it doesn't, try tapping GO LIVE once — the app performs a live status check at that point. Still stuck? Close and reopen the app, or go to Settings → Email Verification and tap to refresh.
